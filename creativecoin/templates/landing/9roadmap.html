THE ROAD MAP

2018

Planning

CISAT planned to create and develop CreativeCoin as medium of exchange in response to the paperless and cashless system for educational processes.

2019-2020

Development & Testing

The CISAT IT Team started in developing and designing the white paper and road map of the CreativeCoin, the creation of the promotional website, the digital wallet app and the blockchain technology.

2020

Revision, redesigning

Due to the effect of the ECQ and lockdown implemented by the governemnt the deployment and testing of the CreativeCoin was moved to more months.

2020

Deployment & Beta Testing

This is the beta testing of the CreativeCoin through registration and mobile app activation.

2020, September

Usage and Purpose

The period and scheduling of usage and function of the CreativeCoin as medium of exchange and payment for tuition fees and enrollment.

2020, December

Introduction of Merchants

CISAT as the major developer of the CreativeCoin will introduce the e-commerce platforms where the said digital asset can be used as medium of exchange and payment for products and services.

2021

Affiliate and Multi-level

The affilate and multi-level marketing programs will introduce to a bigger investment programs to be known publicly. This will be the ICO and IPO
