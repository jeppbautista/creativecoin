MOBILE APP ACTIVATION

To activate the CreativeCoin Mining App, the verified user must be qualified to download the Mobile App from his mobile phone. Verified user will become certified miner if he has purchased a HASHPOWER from the Web Application of CCN. Once the CCN Mining App is activated, a simplified POW and POS will take effect within a period of 60 minutes of each mining block, where one unit of CCN will be generated and recorded automatically in the blockchain, which is the reward of the miner or user, Whatever units of CCN the miner mined, will be considered a simplified POS.
